Cross Reference

I conducted user testing to see what features potential users would want to have in the app. After gathering the research I applied all that I learned to be able to make a satisfactory app.

With choosing the best typography, color pallette and amount of whitespace, I completed the designs with all that I learned from my schooling. Making sure my icons and design elements were pixel perfect and visually appealing. I also practiced visual hierarchy and made sure that the right things were emphasized.

Objective

To be able to cut through the confusion and difficulty of reading the bible, providing study tools to help the user find the correct, in context, interpretations of scripture having the bible as its own interpreter.

Context

There are similar apps such as (Blue Letter Bible, Logos, and The Study Bible), but the key distinguishers of ‘Cross Reference’ is that it provides video links, a clean bookmarking system, and overall easier and cleaner to navigate.

Users

From Pastors to lay persons, from new to the bible to seasoned, believer and non believer. There is no specific target audience other than for those who appreciate easy navigation, clean UI, and want to read the Bible

Key Features

  1. View cross references in the bible Clicking on numbered tab near a word or phrase)
  2. Quick view of original language
  3. When word is clicked
  4. Dropdown tab appears
  5. View videos teaching on specific concepts videos menu(video icon near a concept in the text)
